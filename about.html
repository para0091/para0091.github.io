I'm Fuad.
Nice to meet you.

I’m Fuad Saneen, a student of the Interactive Media Design program at Algonquin College, Ottawa. I was born and raised in Kannur, Kerala. I have tinkered with computers and mobile phones as long as I can remember.

As I became more familiar with interfaces, my curiosity grew and I started learning about how everything worked on mobile and web. Meanwhile, I was also learning a lot about Adobe products and playing around with them.

As I got more experienced in Graphic Design, I started to lean more into Interface and Expperience Design. From there on, I have been solving problems by creating useful and usable interfaces and experiences.

More About Me

I’ve had the opportunity to work on several regional and international projects including the mighty Daily Mail Group. I’m learning more and more by the day and I’m ready for any freelance work that comes my way. When I’m not designing, you can find me watching football, binging football videos on YouTube, or playing football.

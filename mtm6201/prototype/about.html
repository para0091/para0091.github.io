About Us

Nutricones is an ice-cream shop founded by a single mom in 2011. Our special ice-creams have a variety of flavours, each of them are for a different mood and emotion. We provide delicious ice-creams while being healthy and nutritionally conscious.

Our Mission

We are an ice-cream brand accessible to people of all age-groups. We also strive to incorporate the enjoyment and happiness of having ice-creams with the family into the community.

Our Vision

We want our ice-creams to spark immense joy for kids and parents alike. For that, we are currently focusing on the local market, and delivering our products in a small area.
